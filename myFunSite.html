Corona Testing For All

An organization aimed to assist in diagnosis in order to prevent the spread of COVID 19. Tests and procedures approved by WHO

A World Wide Epidemic

We are here to help in a trying time.

Our mission is to provide testing for COVID 19 for little to no cost around the world. Throught the diagnosis of this virus, people will be able to self-isolate and prevent further spreading.

Greater access to testing will help people get tested sooner. The sooner the better. People being tested also helps friends and family to know if they should self-quarantine as well. Overall, we aim to help decrease the spread of COVID 19. One test at a time.
